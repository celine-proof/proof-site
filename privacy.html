Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

proof. takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect
  • Username (required for account creation)
  • Email address (optional — only stored if you provide it, used solely for password recovery if you forget your password)
  • Information you choose to enter, including meal logs, thoughts, sensations, urges, and reappraisals
  • Emotion check-in data
  • Fear food entries and challenge attempts
  • App preferences (such as notification settings and saved coping tools)
What We Do Not Collect
  • We do not intentionally collect calorie counts, weights, or body measurements
  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not collect health data from Apple Health
  • We do not sell your personal data in exchange for monetary compensation
How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide, maintain, and improve the proof. experience. Your entries are stored in your account and are primarily accessible to you. Authorized service providers may access data only as necessary to operate, maintain, or improve the service.

Data Storage

Data is stored using third-party infrastructure providers (such as Supabase) and is encrypted in transit and at rest.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information in accordance with applicable law, including Massachusetts data security regulations (201 CMR 17.00). However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

proof. is not a covered entity or business associate under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), and the app is not intended for storing medical records or protected health information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share information with service providers who assist in operating the app, subject to confidentiality obligations.

We may also use or share anonymized, aggregated data for research or analytics in a way that cannot reasonably identify you.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at celine@proofrecoveryapp.com. We will process requests within a reasonable timeframe, generally within 30 days.

Children

proof. is not intended for users under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it promptly. Contact celine@proofrecoveryapp.comif you believe a child has created an account.
